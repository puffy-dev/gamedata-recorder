## How It Works (3 steps)

### 1. Download & Install
One-click installer. No account needed to start. Under 50 MB.

### 2. Play Normally
GameData Recorder runs silently in the background. It detects when you play a game and starts recording automatically. You won't notice it.

### 3. Earn Money
Your gameplay data is uploaded when you're on WiFi. AI companies pay for this data to train their world models. You get paid.

## What We Record

- Game video (1080p, 30fps) — what's on screen while playing
- Input data — keyboard, mouse, and controller actions
- Game metadata — which game, genre, session duration

**What we DON'T record:**
- Your desktop or browser
- Passwords, banking, or personal info
- Anything outside of games
- Audio or voice chat

## FAQ

**Is this safe?**
Yes. The code is open source — anyone can verify what it does. We use standard Windows APIs, no hooks or injection.

**Will it slow down my games?**
No. We use your GPU's dedicated video encoder (NVENC, AMD AMF, Intel QSV). Typical impact is less than 1 FPS.

**Which games work?**
Most PC games. The recorder auto-detects Steam, Epic, and other games. See the full list.

**When do I get paid?**
Earnings are calculated after each upload. Payouts are monthly via PayPal or direct deposit.

**Can I stop recording?**
Yes. Right-click the tray icon and select "Pause" or "Quit" at any time.
